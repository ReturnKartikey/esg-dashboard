## Brand & Style: Breathe ESG Obsidian Glass

This design system is built to evoke environmental clarity and audit-grade authority in a sophisticated dark mode environment. The brand personality is precise, modern, and clean.

The design style utilizes **Glassmorphism** combined with **luminous semantic color systems** to create infinite spatial depth on dashboard layouts. All panel interfaces utilize semi-transparent card containers with thin, bright, low-opacity borders that define layout bounds without creating heavy borders.

## Colors

- **Base Canvas:** Deep obsidian space black (#080b11) and deep card layers (#0f131c).
- **Scope 1 (Direct Combustion):** Emerald Green (#10b981) represents fuels and material operations.
- **Scope 2 (Indirect Grid):** Electric Cyan (#0ea5e9) represents grid electricity and facilities.
- **Scope 3 (Indirect Value Chain):** Indigo Purple (#8b5cf6) represents flights, lodging, and logistics.
- **Semantic Warnings:** Suspicious records use HSL Warm Amber (#f59e0b). Rejected records use Soft Crimson (#f43f5e).

## Typography

- **Headlines & KPI Totals:** **Outfit** (bold geometric sans-serif) for high-impact figures.
- **Metadata & Data Columns:** **Plus Jakarta Sans** (humanist sans-serif) to ensure reader comfort during deep audit grid validations.
- **Audit Logs & IDs:** **JetBrains Mono** for monospaced traceability in timestamps and hashes.
